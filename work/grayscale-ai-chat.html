Conversational High-Volume Hiring at Grayscale

Context

Grayscale is a hiring platform that integrates with customers’ ATS to automate high-volume recruiting tasks. At its heart lives an AI Assistant named Gracie.

From keeping candidates warm by texting them directly, Gracie quickly expanded to support the full application process through SMS, WhatsApp, and web. I collaborated with Product & Engineering to deliver her rapidly evolving capabilities to end users as quickly as possible.

My Role & Impact

As Grayscale's sole designer, I was responsible for all the design work required to ship new features for Gracie, which drove measurable outcomes reported by customers.

89%

High-volume candidate conversations automated for a major customer.

3x

Increase in recruiter capacity for customers on average.

25%

Reduction in dropoff from application start-to-finish.

High-Level Challenges

Document Uploads

Candidates couldn’t upload documents like resumes directly to Gracie. Redirecting them to external links interrupted the conversation & increased the risk of dropoff.

EEOC Questionnaires

To comply with federal hiring regulations, Gracie needed to present an EEOC questionnaire during the conversational application flow.

Platform Parity

Most frontline candidates engaged with Gracie through SMS and WhatsApp, so features needed to be delivered consistently across both desktop and mobile web.

With no established UX norms for AI jobseeking, this design space was quite open.

Discovery

Because Grayscale prioritized rapid shipping, my capacity to conduct formal research was limited. I turned to job-hunting forums, articles, and competitive analysis to gauge how candidates perceived AI-driven hiring processes.

I learned that many candidates were mistrustful of AI, fearing their applications might never reach a human recruiter. To address this, I designed Gracie’s UI and chat prompt to reassure candidates that their applications would be seen by a human and considered fairly.

Solution

Impact

My design allowed candidates to complete EEOC surveys securely across all channels, while preserving conversational flow and strengthening trust in Gracie. This work helped Grayscale to maintain compliance at a massive scale, and contributed to improved completion rates.
