Visual Synthesizer

Hardware Prototyping, Computer Graphics



Overview

Within a 2-week period and without prior experience with robotics, I challenged myself to design and develop a Visual Synthesizer.

The goal was to explore the potential for playfulness in an arguably logic-oriented world of computing and robotics.

Background

Exploring the possibilities of coding as a creative medium.

This project was inspired by the hobby technology, toys, and video games that brought me joy during my childhood. The hardware design draws from the function and aesthetic of old MIDI synthesizers while the software is inspired by video game character generators.


Designing tangible interactions.

Like a MIDI synthesizer does with sound samples, the Visual Synthesizer allows users to twist knobs and push buttons to adjust the geometric proportions of the default face pictured in Fig 03.


A relaxing video demo

The visual synthesizer can be used to generate a virtually-endless array of unique faces on the screen.
