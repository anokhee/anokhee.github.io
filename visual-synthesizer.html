What is this?

Within a 2-week period and without prior experience with DIY robotics or the Processing programming language, I challenged myself to design and develop a Visual Synthesizer.

Fig 01. Hardware Input Device

Background

As I explore the possibilities of coding as a creative medium, I find myself coming back to the things that brought me joy during my childhood. The hardware aspect of this project draws from the function and aesthetic of old MIDI synthesizers while the software is inspired by video game character generators.

Like a MIDI synthesizer does with sound samples, the Visual Synthesizer allows users to twist knobs and push buttons to manipulate the geometric proportions of a default face to generate a variety of new and unique faces.
