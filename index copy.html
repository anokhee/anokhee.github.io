Landing Page at iLearn

Web Design, Illustration

Context

Overview

iLearn is a mathematics e-learning solution for K-12, college, and at-home use, with the purpose of filling gaps in students’ knowledge of common core K-12 math.

My primary duties involved testing the product for quality assurance, but I was able to explore my design interests by contributing to the updated landing page.

Team: User Experience
Tools: Adobe Creative Suite
Timeline: September 2015 - March 2016

Problem

The majority of visitors to iLearn’s site spent only a few seconds on the homepage before navigating away.

1) iLearn's homepage was not optimized for SEO.

iLearn's homepage was not configured for SEO, attracting clicks from outside the desired demographic, such as teachers, students and administrators looking for software to supplement high-level math courses or outside of K-12 education.

2) Users couldn't find the information they needed.

The current homepage design was not accurately or adequately conveying information about iLearn's services.

3) Outdated site UI reflected unfavorably upon iLearn as a brand.
