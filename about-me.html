I am a UX Designer living in Atlanta, but I was born in Botswana: a beautiful country in southern Africa with the 12th-worst Internet connection in the world. And I've been obsessed with computers for as long as I can remember.

Growing up, I loved computers for the creative potential they afforded me. When printer paper seemed more valuable to my dad than gold, I made endless drawings in MS Paint. I learned how to spell my name by typing it on the keyboard. I explored my love for music through a virus-infested MIDI sequencing software called Babya Logic that killed the family computer.

I am a "cynical optimist" when it comes to technology. I believe that the best technologies are those which create opportunities and enhance our real lives and relationships rather than distract us from them. As a designer, I try to breathe this sentiment into everything I do.
