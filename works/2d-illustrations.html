Duck in the Sky

This is a portrait of a bird in the sky, gliding around on thin air with his pet fish and octopus inside his shoes. All he wants to do is show them the world as he sees and loves it, but they're terrified. From their perspective, they're being senselessly thrashed around in a small tank. They just want to go home.

Created using Procreate on an iPad Pro & Apple Pencil.

Home on the Range

I drew this with "Good Intentions Paving Company" by Joanna Newsom and "Horse With No Name" by America buzzing around in my head. In my mind, this sandal-wearing, sunburnt person has finally found peace living as equals with an anonymous wild animal that doesn't want to be known or loved, much less tamed by a human.

Created using Adobe Illustrator for MacOS.
